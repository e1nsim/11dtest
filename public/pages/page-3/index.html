Directory of Toilets in Public Parks

This website is a comprehensive guide to public park toilets located in the different boroughs of New York City. The homepage is designed to resemble a map of the city, with circles representing each borough placed in their relative location. The circles also reflect the sizes of the boroughs in relation to each other. When visitors click on each circle, they are taken to a page that lists the public park toilets available in that borough. The list is organized into a chart that includes the name of each park, its location, and the number of toilets available. Visitors can easily search for toilets in their area or explore different neighborhoods and parks.
