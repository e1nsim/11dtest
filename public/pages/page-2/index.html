Luxury Candles

This website is dedicated to luxury candles that are grouped into four categories: Daily, Relaxation, Date, and Winter. The homepage features an introduction to the website and the different categories of candles available. Visitors can click on each category to explore the different scents and styles offered.Each category has its own page with a selection of candles and descriptions of their scents and benefits. The candles are organized into columns that resemble perfume swatches.
